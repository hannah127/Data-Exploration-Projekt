Use-case

Knowing whether a student has accademic success or will dropout can be of useful knowledge. 
It can be interesting for the student himself to understand his possibilities of success when making the decision to go to university or not.
Moreover, the knowledge can be useful for companies as well. It is escpecially important when a decision is about to be made if they 
want to employe a students as working studens. Having this information the economic success could be greater because a company has not
invested in a student which will not have a degree and will not be a full-time in the future.
